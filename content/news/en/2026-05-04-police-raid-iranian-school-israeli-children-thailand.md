# Thai Police Raid Illegal Iranian-Run School for Israeli Kids

In a surprising development in Bangkok, Thai police conducted a raid on an illegal school operated by Iranian nationals that was providing education to Israeli children. The operation took place recently, though exact dates are not specified in available reports. The school was running without proper authorization from Thai education authorities, prompting the intervention to ensure compliance with local laws. This incident underscores ongoing challenges with unregulated educational facilities catering to expatriate communities in Thailand.
