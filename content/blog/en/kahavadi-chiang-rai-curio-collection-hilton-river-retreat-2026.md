Northern Thailand is getting a new boutique luxury property. The Kahavadi Chiang Rai, Curio Collection by Hilton, is set to open on July 15, 2026, along the banks of the Kok River near Chiang Rai's city center. The hotel brings Hilton's design-forward Curio Collection brand to one of Thailand's most culturally rich but under-visited provinces.

## Lanna Heritage Meets Modern Hospitality

The property's design draws inspiration from a traditional Lanna merchant's residence, weaving cultural storytelling into every aspect of the guest experience. Lanna refers to the historic kingdom that once encompassed northern Thailand, and its artistic traditions, temple architecture, and culinary heritage remain deeply embedded in Chiang Rai's identity.

The hotel offers 167 rooms and suites, along with a swimming pool, fitness center, spa facilities, and up to 490 square meters of flexible event space. Opening rates start from approximately 3,553 Thai Baht per night (roughly USD 100), inclusive of a 10 percent service charge but exclusive of taxes.

## Three Distinctive Dining Venues

Food is clearly a priority at the Kahavadi. The hotel features three restaurants, each with its own concept:

- **Larderley** takes inspiration from the traditional Thai pinto (stacked tiffin carrier), offering a communal dining experience that blends Thai, Asian, and international flavors through layered, shareable dishes.
- **Tasabiang** sits along the riverbank and serves Northern Thai cuisine elevated with contemporary techniques. The menu relies on seasonal ingredients sourced from the surrounding Chiang Rai ecosystem.
- **The Ledger** is a cocktail lounge inspired by vintage retail ledgers, serving signature cocktails alongside flavorful small plates with globally influenced ingredients.

## Why Chiang Rai Deserves Your Attention

Chiang Rai is often overshadowed by its more famous neighbor, Chiang Mai, but the province has been steadily building its tourism profile. It is home to the iconic White Temple (Wat Rong Khun), the Blue Temple, and the Baan Dam Museum, as well as hill tribe communities and lush tea plantations. The addition of Scoot's direct flights from Singapore in January 2026 has already improved international access.

The Kahavadi's opening adds a much-needed upscale accommodation option for travelers who want to explore northern Thailand beyond Chiang Mai. With Hilton Honors integration, loyalty program members can earn and redeem points, making the property accessible to frequent Hilton guests.

## Booking Information

Reservations are already open through Hilton's website for stays from July 15, 2026 onward. Travelers planning a northern Thailand itinerary later this year now have a strong reason to extend their trip from Chiang Mai to Chiang Rai.
