## What Is HYROX?

HYROX is a global fitness racing series that combines running with functional workout stations. Competitors complete eight one-kilometre running laps, each followed by a different exercise station covering sled pushes, rowing, wall balls, burpee broad jumps and more. Think of it as a marathon meets CrossFit, open to athletes of every level from casual gym-goers to elite competitors.

The format has exploded in popularity worldwide, and [Bangkok](/city/bangkok/) is now a flagship stop on the Asian calendar. If you are into fitness travel in Thailand, the scene extends well beyond HYROX — from [Muay Thai training camps](/blog/muay-thai-training-camps-thailand-beginners-guide-2026/) to [wellness retreats across the country](/blog/best-wellness-retreats-thailand-2026/).

## HYROX Bangkok 2026 at a Glance

The BYD HYROX Bangkok 2026 took place from **20 to 22 March** at the **Bangkok International Trade & Exhibition Centre (BITEC)** in Bang Na. This year's edition shattered attendance records with over **17,500 registered athletes**, nearly doubling the 8,700 participants from 2025 and making it the largest HYROX race ever staged in [Bangkok](/city/bangkok/).

Five competition categories were available: Singles, Singles Pro, Doubles, Doubles Pro, and Relay Teams. An Adaptive category also provided a platform for athletes with permanent functional impairments, reinforcing the event's inclusive ethos. Bangkok's growing reputation as a [sports and events destination](/blog/bangkok-best-city-asia-2026-destinasian-award/) has helped attract major international competitions like this to the city.

## The Atmosphere Beyond the Race

HYROX is not just for competitors. The event featured **HYROX House**, a festival-style area with curated food and beverage stalls, live DJ sets, brand activations and activities for families and spectators. Thai celebrity Chompoo Araya drew headlines by completing her HYROX debut in a strong showing, boosting the sport's visibility in Thailand. Bangkok's events calendar is packed — from [K-pop concerts](/blog/kpop-concerts-bangkok-2026-seventeen-treasure-bts-guide/) to [the Bangkok Art Biennale](/blog/bangkok-art-biennale-2026-angels-mara-guide/), making any visit worthwhile even if you are not racing.

## Getting to BITEC

BITEC sits directly at **BTS Bang Na station** on the Sukhumvit Line, making it easy to reach from central Bangkok. If you are unfamiliar with the city's rail network, the [Bangkok BTS & MRT tourist guide](/blog/bangkok-public-transport-bts-mrt-tourist-guide-2026/) covers all lines and fares in detail. Taxis and Grab rides from Sukhumvit hotspots typically take 20 to 40 minutes depending on traffic.

**Address:** 88 Debaratna Road, Bang Na Tai, Bang Na, Bangkok 10260.

For accommodation close to the event, check the [best hotels in Bangkok](/best-hotels/bangkok/) or read the [Bangkok neighborhood guide](/blog/where-to-stay-bangkok-neighborhood-guide/) to pick the right area for your stay.

## Planning for the Next Edition

If you missed the 2026 race, registration for the next Bangkok edition typically opens six to eight months in advance on the official HYROX website. Early-bird slots sell out fast — the 2026 race reached capacity well before event day. Keep an eye on the HYROX Thailand social channels for announcements.
