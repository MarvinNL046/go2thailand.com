## What Is HYROX?

HYROX is a global fitness racing series that combines running with functional workout stations. Competitors complete eight one-kilometre running laps, each followed by a different exercise station covering sled pushes, rowing, wall balls, burpee broad jumps and more. Think of it as a marathon meets CrossFit, open to athletes of every level from casual gym-goers to elite competitors.

The format has exploded in popularity worldwide, and Bangkok is now a flagship stop on the Asian calendar.

## HYROX Bangkok 2026 at a Glance

The BYD HYROX Bangkok 2026 took place from **20 to 22 March** at the **Bangkok International Trade & Exhibition Centre (BITEC)** in Bang Na. This year's edition shattered attendance records with over **17,500 registered athletes**, nearly doubling the 8,700 participants from 2025 and making it the largest HYROX race ever staged in Bangkok.

Five competition categories were available: Singles, Singles Pro, Doubles, Doubles Pro, and Relay Teams. An Adaptive category also provided a platform for athletes with permanent functional impairments, reinforcing the event's inclusive ethos.

## The Atmosphere Beyond the Race

HYROX is not just for competitors. The event featured **HYROX House**, a festival-style area with curated food and beverage stalls, live DJ sets, brand activations and activities for families and spectators. Thai celebrity Chompoo Araya drew headlines by completing her HYROX debut in a strong showing, boosting the sport's visibility in Thailand.

## Getting to BITEC

BITEC sits directly at **BTS Bang Na station** on the Sukhumvit Line, making it easy to reach from central Bangkok. Taxis and Grab rides from Sukhumvit hotspots typically take 20 to 40 minutes depending on traffic.

**Address:** 88 Debaratna Road, Bang Na Tai, Bang Na, Bangkok 10260.

## Planning for the Next Edition

If you missed the 2026 race, registration for the next Bangkok edition typically opens six to eight months in advance on the official HYROX website. Early-bird slots sell out fast — the 2026 race reached capacity well before event day. Keep an eye on the HYROX Thailand social channels for announcements.

Whether you are a seasoned fitness competitor or just curious about the global racing craze sweeping Bangkok, HYROX is one of the most energising events on Thailand's sports calendar.
