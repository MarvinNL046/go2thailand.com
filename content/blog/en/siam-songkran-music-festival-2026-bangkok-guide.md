## Songkran Meets World-Class EDM

Thailand's Songkran water festival is already one of the wildest celebrations in Southeast Asia, but Bangkok has been steadily turning it into a global music event as well. The Siam Songkran Music Festival returns in April 2026 with a lineup that includes headliners Martin Garrix and Marshmello, alongside a packed card of international and Thai DJs.

The festival combines the traditional Songkran water fights with large-scale electronic dance music stages, creating an experience that is part cultural celebration, part open-air rave.

## Dates and Location

The Siam Songkran Music Festival 2026 takes place during the official Songkran holiday period in mid-April in Bangkok. The event is typically held at a large outdoor venue in the city centre, with multiple stages, food vendors, and dedicated water zones.

Separately, Silom Road hosts its own massive free Songkran celebration from April 12 to 14. The Silom Edge event this year includes a free live concert by Hai Apaporn Nakhonsawan on April 13 from 4pm to 5pm, well-known DJs from BEEF.BKK, a central dance zone, street food stalls, and free water refill points.

## What to Expect at the Festival

Siam Songkran is not a typical music festival where you stand dry in a field. Water is a central part of the experience. Expect to be soaked within minutes of arriving, which is entirely the point during Thailand's hottest month of the year.

The festival grounds include multiple music stages with different genres and vibes, dedicated areas for water play and foam parties, food and drink vendors serving Thai street food and cocktails, and VIP zones with elevated viewing platforms.

Previous editions have drawn tens of thousands of attendees from Thailand and across the region, and the 2026 lineup suggests this year will be no different.

## Tickets and Practical Tips

Tickets for Siam Songkran are available through the official website and platforms like KKday and Klook. Prices vary by tier, with early-bird and VIP options typically selling out fast. If you are planning to attend, buying tickets well in advance is recommended.

A few practical tips for festival-goers. Wear quick-dry clothing and waterproof sandals. Use a waterproof phone pouch, as water cannons do not discriminate. Arrive early to avoid long queues at the entrance. Stay hydrated, because April in Bangkok regularly exceeds 35 degrees Celsius.

## Beyond the Main Event

If Siam Songkran is not your style, Bangkok offers plenty of other Songkran experiences during the same period. Khaosan Road has its legendary backpacker water fight, Silom Road hosts the free street party mentioned above, and the S2O Songkran Music Festival offers another EDM-focused option with a different lineup. Between all of these events, Songkran 2026 in Bangkok is shaping up to be one of the best yet.
