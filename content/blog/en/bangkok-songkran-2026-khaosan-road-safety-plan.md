Bangkok officials have agreed on a joint safety plan for this year's **[Songkran celebrations on Khaosan Road](/blog/songkran-festival-2026-guide/)**, one of the most popular [water festival](/blog/songkran-2026-76-provinces-nationwide-celebrations-guide/) spots for international visitors. With Songkran 2026 running from **April 11 to 17**, multiple agencies are coordinating to ensure a safer festival experience.

## What's New This Year

The plan represents the most comprehensive safety approach for Khaosan Road's Songkran celebrations to date, addressing lessons learned from previous years when overcrowding and alcohol-related incidents marred the festivities. Bangkok has also earned recognition as [best city in Asia](/blog/bangkok-best-city-asia-2026-destinasian-award/), making festival safety a high-profile priority for the capital.

### Security and Crowd Control

**CCTV cameras** will monitor the area 24 hours a day throughout the festival period. Screening points will be set up at entry zones, and emergency exits have been designated to help disperse crowds if density becomes too high.

Medical support teams, first-aid stations, and ambulances will be positioned across the area. Staff will be deployed to maintain safety and assist visitors. The [Siam Songkran Music Festival](/blog/siam-songkran-music-festival-2026-bangkok-guide/) and the [S2O EDM water party](/blog/s2o-songkran-music-festival-2026-bangkok-edm-water-party/) are also operating nearby under similar safety frameworks.

### Traffic Management

Officials have agreed to set up a **dedicated service lane** for emergency vehicle access. Entry and exit routes will be clearly marked to reduce crowd build-up around the water festivities. Direction signs and information notices will be installed at regular intervals in both Thai and English. Visitors arriving by [public transport via BTS and MRT](/blog/bangkok-public-transport-bts-mrt-tourist-guide-2026/) will find dedicated crowd-flow lanes from the nearest stations.

## The "5 Por" Campaign

Authorities are promoting the **"5 Por" campaign** (five prohibitions), calling for a Songkran celebration free from:

1. **[High-pressure water guns](/blog/songkran-2026-water-gun-rules-fines-safety-guide/)** that can cause injury
2. **Alcohol** in the festival zone
3. **Nudity** or inappropriate clothing
4. **Powder** (talcum throwing)
5. **Weapons** of any kind

## Road Safety: The "Seven Dangerous Days"

The Songkran period is traditionally known as the **"Seven Dangerous Days"** due to a sharp spike in road accidents across Thailand. The "Safe Songkran" initiative means **24/7 DUI checkpoints** and vehicle seizures for drunk drivers. For the latest travel safety advice for the country, see [is Thailand safe for tourists in 2026](/blog/is-thailand-safe-tourists-2026/).

If you're visiting [Bangkok](/best-hotels/bangkok/) for Songkran, public transport is strongly recommended. The BTS Skytrain and MRT are the safest and most efficient ways to reach Khaosan Road and other celebration areas. The new [Yellow and Pink Line monorails](/blog/bangkok-yellow-pink-line-monorail-tourist-guide-2026/) also connect outer Bangkok to the festival zones.

- **Arrive early**: Khaosan Road gets extremely crowded after noon
- **Use a waterproof phone pouch**: essential for protecting your valuables
- **Wear quick-dry clothing and water shoes**: you will get soaked
- **Stay hydrated**: the [April heat](/blog/el-nino-2026-thailand-weather-heatwave-travel-tips/) combined with water fights is exhausting

- **Respect the culture**: Songkran is a religious holiday at its core, so be mindful of [monks and temple ceremonies](/blog/thailand-temple-etiquette-dress-code-guide-2026/)
- **Book accommodation early**: explore [where to stay in Bangkok by neighbourhood](/blog/where-to-stay-bangkok-neighborhood-guide/) before options sell out over the festival period
- **Consider alternative celebrations**: [Phra Pradaeng](/blog/phra-pradaeng-songkran-2026-mon-culture-samut-prakan/) near Samut Prakan hosts a quieter Mon-culture Songkran if you prefer something away from the crowds
- **Discounted flights**: [Songkran airline deals](/blog/songkran-2026-airlines-discount-flights/) are available if you haven't booked travel yet
- **Silom is another option**: the [Silom Edge Songkran](/blog/silom-edge-songkran-2026-bangkok-water-festival-event/) event offers a more contained water festival experience in the CBD
