A small island just 15 minutes by speedboat from [Phuket](/city/phuket/) has claimed a major prize. **Banana Beach on Koh Hey** (also known as Coral Island) has been named **Asia's most beautiful beach** in Tripadvisor's 2026 Travelers' Choice Awards: Best of the Best Destinations. If you're planning a visit, our [Phuket travel guide](/blog/phuket-travel-guide-2026/) covers everything you need to know about the island.

## Why Banana Beach Won

Tripadvisor's ranking is based on the quality and quantity of traveler reviews and ratings collected over the past year. Banana Beach stood out for its powdery white sand, crystal-clear turquoise water, and vibrant coral reefs just offshore. Visitors consistently praise the [snorkeling](/best-diving-snorkeling-in-thailand/) — you can spot colorful fish and healthy coral formations without venturing far from the beach.

The beach sits in a sheltered cove on Koh Hey's western side, which keeps the water calm and visibility high during the peak season from November through April.

## What You'll Find There

Banana Beach is relatively compact compared to [Phuket's main beaches](/best-beaches-in-thailand/), which adds to its charm. Facilities include:

- **Hornbill Restaurant** serving Thai and international dishes right on the sand
- **Sun lounger and umbrella rentals** (prices vary by season; expect to pay around 200–300 baht)
- **Snorkeling gear rental** available on-site
- **Parasailing and kayaking** for those who want more than just swimming

The beach does get busy during peak hours (roughly 11:00–14:00) when day-trip boats arrive from Phuket. For a quieter experience, arrive early in the morning or stay past mid-afternoon. Check the [best beach clubs in Phuket](/blog/phuket-best-beach-clubs-2026-yona-catch-barra-cuda/) if you want to combine your trip with a full beach-club day.

## How to Get There

Most visitors reach Koh Hey from **Chalong Pier** in southern Phuket. Speedboats take about 15 minutes and cost around 800–1,500 baht for a round-trip day tour, depending on the operator and inclusions. Longtail boats are cheaper but take longer (30–40 minutes).

Several [Phuket](/city/phuket/top-10-attractions/)-based tour operators offer half-day and full-day packages that include snorkeling stops, lunch, and beach time on Koh Hey. You can also arrange a private longtail boat for more flexibility. If you want to combine this trip with island hopping, our [Thailand island hopping guide](/blog/thailand-island-hopping-guide/) is a great starting point — nearby options include the [Phi Phi Islands](/city/phuket/attractions/phi-phi-islands/) and [Koh Lipe](/islands/koh-lipe/).

## Good to Know Before You Go

- **Best months:** November through April, when seas are calm and skies are mostly clear — see our [best time to visit Thailand](/blog/best-time-to-visit-thailand/) guide for full seasonal advice
- **National park fee:** Koh Hey is not inside a national park, so there is no entrance fee for the island itself — though some tour packages include fees for nearby snorkeling spots that are
- **Bring cash:** card payment options on the island are limited
- **Reef-safe sunscreen** is strongly recommended to help protect the coral that earned this beach its reputation
