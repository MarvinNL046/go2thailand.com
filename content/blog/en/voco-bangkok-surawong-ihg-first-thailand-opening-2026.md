## IHG Brings Its Fastest-Growing Premium Brand to Bangkok

IHG Hotels & Resorts has officially opened voco Bangkok Surawong, marking the debut of the voco brand in Thailand. The 244-room property sits along Surawong Road in the heart of Bangkok's Silom district and is now welcoming guests.

voco is IHG's fastest-growing premium brand worldwide. Choosing Bangkok as the first Thai location signals the company's confidence in the city's continued appeal to upscale leisure and business travellers alike.

## A Brutalist Landmark Reimagined

The building has history. It previously operated as the Tawana Hotel, one of Bangkok's well-known mid-century properties. Award-winning Thai architecture firm A49 led the redesign, preserving the original Brutalist modernism while introducing natural light, open sightlines and contemporary interior flow.

The result is a hotel that feels both familiar and fresh — geometric concrete forms sit alongside warm wood finishes and lush garden courtyards. Guests who remember the old Tawana will recognise the bones of the building, but the experience is entirely new.

## Dining and Amenities

Three restaurants and bars anchor the food and drink offering:

- **Tasca Sabio** — a Spanish-inspired restaurant bringing Iberian flavours to Surawong Road
- **Deckles Smokehouse** — an all-day dining venue built around a premium smokehouse concept, a first for a Bangkok hotel of this category

Other amenities include a full lap pool surrounded by landscaped gardens, a 24-hour fitness centre and an onsen-style spa.

A small detail worth mentioning: every guest receives a mango sticky rice cookie at check-in. The hotel's pastry chef prepares them daily using fresh local mangoes — a modern nod to one of Thailand's most beloved desserts.

## Location

Surawong Road puts guests within walking distance of three distinct Bangkok neighbourhoods. Silom's street food and nightlife are steps away. Sathorn's corporate towers are a short taxi ride south. And the BTS Skytrain connects the area to Siam's shopping malls in under ten minutes.

## Who Is It For?

voco Bangkok Surawong sits in the premium tier — above a standard Holiday Inn but below a full InterContinental. It is a strong option for travellers who want a centrally located hotel with design character and reliable IHG loyalty benefits, without paying true luxury prices. Room rates at launch start from around 4,000 THB per night.
