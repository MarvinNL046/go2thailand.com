## Electronic Music Legends Come to Bangkok

Kraftwerk, the German group widely credited with shaping electronic music as we know it, will perform in [Bangkok](https://go2-thailand.com/city/bangkok/) on Sunday, May 10, 2026. It is the first time the band has ever played in Thailand, and one of only two Southeast Asian stops on their current Multimedia World Tour.

The show takes place at the Queen Sirikit National Convention Center (QSNCC) in the Khlong Toei district of Bangkok — easily reachable via the MRT (Queen Sirikit National Convention Center station). QSNCC is the same venue that hosts the annual [Thailand Travel Fair](https://go2-thailand.com/blog/thailand-travel-fair-2026-qsncc-bangkok/) and other major Bangkok events.

## What Is the Multimedia Tour?

Kraftwerk's live shows are not standard concerts. The group performs behind matching consoles while a full-venue 3D visual system projects animations, geometric patterns, and archival footage in sync with the music. Sound, light, and video operate as a single precisely engineered system, turning the audience space into an immersive audiovisual environment.

The setlist typically draws from more than four decades of material, including tracks from Autobahn, The Man-Machine, Computer World, and Tour de France.

## Why It Matters for Bangkok

[Bangkok](https://go2-thailand.com/city/bangkok/)'s live music scene has grown rapidly over the past few years, with major international acts increasingly adding the city to their Asian tour routes. Kraftwerk's debut here follows a trend that has already seen K-pop groups, EDM headliners, and legacy rock bands choosing QSNCC and other Bangkok venues over more traditional stops like Tokyo or Singapore. Bangkok was even named [best city in Asia for 2026](https://go2-thailand.com/blog/bangkok-best-city-asia-2026-destinasian-award/) by DestinAsian.

For travellers visiting Thailand in May 2026, the concert offers a strong reason to spend an extra night in Bangkok before heading to the islands or the north. May is also when the [Royal Ploughing Ceremony](https://go2-thailand.com/blog/royal-ploughing-ceremony-bangkok-may-2026/) takes place — another unique Bangkok event worth combining with your trip.
