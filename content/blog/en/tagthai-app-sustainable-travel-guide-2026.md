## What Is TAGTHAi?

TAGTHAi — short for Thailand All-in-One Guide — is a travel platform backed by the Tourism Authority of Thailand (TAT). Available on both iOS and Android, the app bundles hotel bookings, attraction tickets, restaurant deals and curated travel routes into a single interface. Think of it as Thailand's official answer to the scattered mix of booking apps most tourists juggle on holiday.

The platform now connects over 3,000 local partners, from boutique guesthouses and hidden-gem restaurants to luxury spas and traditional craft workshops. In 2025 alone, TAGTHAi saw a 100 percent increase in downloads, surpassing four million, and a 170 percent jump in purchases.

## What Changed in 2026

Two big upgrades arrived this year:

### AI-Powered Trip Planning

TAGTHAi introduced AI-driven recommendations that build personalised itineraries based on your interests, budget and travel dates. The system learns from past user behaviour across the platform to suggest routes that balance popular highlights with lesser-known stops.

### EcoVenture Experience Passes

The new "EcoVenture" pass is a curated route designed to encourage responsible travel in secondary cities. These are the smaller Thai towns and villages that rarely appear on mainstream itineraries but offer authentic cultural experiences. Each pass bundles transport, accommodation and activities at a discount while channelling revenue directly to local communities.

The idea is simple: spread tourism income beyond Bangkok, Phuket and Chiang Mai and give travellers a reason to explore Thailand's quieter corners.

## How It Supports Sustainable Tourism

TAGTHAi's green push aligns with a broader TAT strategy. In 2026, the authority is working toward having 70 percent of Thai tourism businesses accredited under the STAR rating system (Sustainable Tourism Acceleration Rating) by year's end.

Through TAGTHAi, travellers can:

- **Filter for eco-certified stays** — properties that meet energy, water and waste standards.
- **Book community-based experiences** — homestays, local cooking classes and guided village walks that generate income for rural families.
- **Track their travel footprint** — the app provides estimates of carbon impact for different transport and accommodation choices.

## How to Get Started

1. Download TAGTHAi from the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/tagthai/id1457643150) or [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.thaidigitalplatform.tagthai).
2. Create a free account.
3. Browse Experience Passes or use the AI planner to build a custom itinerary.
4. Pay in-app — most passes include skip-the-line entry at popular attractions.

## Worth Installing?

If you are visiting Thailand in 2026, TAGTHAi is a practical download. The eco-tourism routes are genuinely useful for anyone wanting to travel more responsibly without spending hours researching small-town destinations. And the discounts through bundled passes can save real money compared to booking everything separately.
