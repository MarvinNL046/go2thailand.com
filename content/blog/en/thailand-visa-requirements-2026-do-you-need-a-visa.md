## 1. Understanding Visa-Free Entry to Thailand

**Visa-free entry** lets travelers from over 90 countries skip the embassy hassle for short trips, a game-changer we relied on during our southern island-hops from [Phuket travel guide](https://go2-thailand.com/city/phuket/) to [Koh Samui island guide](https://go2-thailand.com/islands/koh-samui/). In 2026, this scheme remains the simplest path for most tourists, extended to 60 days amid post-pandemic recovery efforts.

During our three years in Chiang Mai, we saw lines shorten dramatically for visa-exempt nationalities, but always double-check your passport's validity—minimum six months required.

### Eligible Countries and Durations

Citizens of the US, UK, EU nations, Australia, Canada, and many more qualify for 60 days visa-free by air or land. Check the full list on the [Thai Immigration Bureau](https://www.immigration.go.th/) site (target="_blank").

- **Air Entry:** 60 days stamp on arrival.
- **Land Entry:** Same 60 days, but report every 90 days if staying longer.
- **Pro Tip:** Fly into Bangkok for smoother processing than land borders near [Chiang Rai](https://go2-thailand.com/city/chiang-rai/).

In our [experience](https://getyourguide.tpo.lv/GuAFfGGK?subid=blog), presenting a return ticket and proof of funds (20,000 THB/person) avoids questions at immigration.

### Changes for 2026

No major overhauls announced, but the 60-day limit holds firm per official updates. We anticipate continuity based on TAT announcements.

> **Did You Know?** Thailand's visa exemption now covers 93 countries, up from 60 pre-2024, boosting tourism recovery.
>
> *Source: [Tourism Authority of Thailand](https://www.tourismthailand.org/)*

## 2. Tourist Visa: When You Need One

If your nationality isn't visa-exempt, the **tourist visa** (TR) is your go-to for 60 days, extendable by 30. We applied for one before extended stays in [Bangkok travel guide](https://go2-thailand.com/city/bangkok/), where bureaucracy felt lighter than rumored.

This visa suits multi-entry trips, perfect for combining northern treks with [southern beaches](https://go2-thailand.com/region/southern/).

### Single vs. Multiple Entry Options

| Option | Best For | Cost | Rating |
|--------|----------|------|--------|
| **Single-Entry (TR)** | One trip up to 60 days | 2,000 THB ($58 USD) | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| **Multiple-Entry (METV)** | 6 months, 60 days/entry | 5,000 THB ($145 USD) | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |

Apply at a Thai embassy or consulate abroad—never in Thailand. Processing takes 3-5 days.

### Step-by-Step Application Process

- **Gather Documents:** Passport (6+ months valid), photo, itinerary, bank statement (20,000 THB min).
- **Budget Option:** Apply in neighboring Laos or Cambodia for faster service.
- **Common Pitfall:** Incomplete forms lead to rejection; use embassy checklists.

When we lived in Chiang Mai, friends flew to Vientiane for theirs—back in 48 hours.

## 3. Thailand Digital Arrival Card (TDAC): Mandatory for Everyone

Starting 2025 and continuing into 2026, the **Thailand Digital Arrival Card** replaces paper TM6 forms for all foreigners. Submit online 3 days before arrival via [tdac.immigration.go.th](https://tdac.immigration.go.th/arrival-card/#/home) (target="_blank")—free and quick.

We filled ours on the plane WiFi during a flight to Phuket, scanning QR at immigration for instant entry.

### How to Complete TDAC

Takes 5 minutes: personal details, flight info, address in Thailand ([hotel](https://booking.tpo.lv/2PT1kR82?subid=blog) ok).

- **Timeline:** 72 hours max before landing.
- **Devices:** Mobile-friendly; save PDF.
- **Insider Hack:** Pre-fill for groups to speed up.

Failure means denial at the gate—don't skip it.

### Linking to Your Stay

Tie it to your [practical info](https://go2-thailand.com/practical-info/) like [packing list](https://go2-thailand.com/practical-info/packing-list/) and [health vaccinations](https://go2-thailand.com/practical-info/health-vaccinations/).

> **Did You Know?** All passengers must complete TDAC; stays over 90 days require 90-day reporting to immigration.
>
> *Source: [Thai Immigration Bureau](https://www.immigration.go.th/)*

## 4. Visa Extensions: Stay Longer Legally

Most visas extend 30 days for 1,900 THB (~$55 USD) at immigration offices. In Bangkok or Chiang Mai, we extended ours hassle-free before heading south.

Avoid overstays—fines start at 500 THB/day.

### Where and How to Extend

Head to the local immigration office (e.g., Chaeng Wattana in Bangkok).

- **Documents:** Passport, TM7 form, photo, 1,900 THB cash.
- **Timing:** Apply 7-15 days before expiry.
- **Best Practice:** Go early morning to beat queues.

During Songkran in April, offices get swamped—plan ahead per our [Songkran survival guide](https://go2-thailand.com/blog/songkran-2026-survival-guide-what-to-know/).

## 5. Visa on Arrival (VOA): Quick Option for Some

**Visa on Arrival** suits 31 nationalities for 15 days (extendable). Pay 2,000 THB at major airports/land borders.

We used it once entering from Laos—efficient but pricier than exemption.

### Eligible Nationalities and Process

List includes India, China, Middle East nations. Queue at dedicated VOA counters.

**Steps:**
1. Fill form, photo, pay.
2. 15 days max; extend for 1,900 THB.

Link your VOA stay to [first time Thailand](https://go2-thailand.com/travel-guides/first-time-thailand/) plans.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do US citizens need a visa for Thailand?
US passport holders get 60 days visa-free by air/land in 2026. Extend at immigration for 1,900 THB if needed. Always complete TDAC 3 days prior.

### How long can I stay in Thailand without a visa?
60 days for eligible nationalities; 15 days VOA for others. Overstays incur 500 THB daily fines. See [Visa Free Entry](https://go2-thailand.com/visa/visa-free-entry/) for details.

### What's the cost of a Thailand tourist visa?
Single-entry: 2,000 THB ($58 USD); multiple: 5,000 THB ($145 USD). Apply at embassies abroad. Extension adds 1,900 THB for 30 days.

### Can I extend my visa-free stay in Thailand?
Yes, 30 days for 1,900 THB at immigration offices. Apply before expiry; read our [Visa Extension](https://go2-thailand.com/visa/visa-extension/) guide.

### Do I need a visa for a 30-[day trip](https://klook.tpo.lv/7Dt6WApj?subid=blog) to Thailand?
Most likely visa-free if from 93 eligible countries. Confirm nationality list on immigration.go.th and submit TDAC.

### What is the Thailand Digital Arrival Card?
Mandatory online form for all arrivals, free, submit 3 days early at tdac.immigration.go.th. Replaces paper TM6; QR scan at airport.

### Can I work on a tourist visa in Thailand?
No, work requires a permit and Non-B visa. Violations lead to fines or bans. Check [expat long stay Thailand](https://go2-thailand.com/travel-guides/expat-long-stay-thailand/).
