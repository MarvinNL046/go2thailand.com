## A New Kind of Museum in Bangkok

Bangkok already has no shortage of temples, markets, and rooftop bars. But in 2026 the city added something genuinely different: Space & Time Cube+, a 1,500-square-metre immersive metaverse museum on the basement floor of Seacon Bangkae shopping centre. If you are into digital art, light installations, or simply looking for a unique afternoon activity, this one deserves a spot on your itinerary.

## What Is Space & Time Cube?

Space & Time Cube+ blends cutting-edge projection technology with art to create environments that feel otherworldly. The museum is divided into three zones containing 27 themed rooms, each designed to transport visitors through imaginative settings that merge ancient and modern elements.

The headline technology is a 720-degree CAVE system — meaning projections surround you from every angle, including above and below. Combined with glasses-free 3D hologram displays, the effect is genuinely immersive without the discomfort of wearing VR headsets for extended periods.

## Highlights Inside

Some of the standout installations include:

- **Stream of Light and Moon Lanterns** — a room where glowing lanterns appear to float around you in an infinite space
- **Ocean of Stars** — an underwater-themed projection room that feels like diving into a bioluminescent sea
- **Golden Waterfall** — a visually stunning cascade of light that reacts to movement
- **9D Film Theatre** — a short motion-ride experience with wind, water spray, and vibrating seats (available as an add-on ticket)

Every room is designed to be photogenic, so expect plenty of opportunities for social media content. The lighting is calibrated for phone cameras, which is a thoughtful touch.

## Practical Information

**Location:** Basement floor, Seacon Bangkae, Bang Khae district, Bangkok. The mall is accessible by taxi or grab; the nearest BTS station is Bang Wa, from which you can take a short taxi ride.

**Opening hours:** Daily, typically 10:00–21:00. Check the venue's social media pages for holiday hours.

**Tickets:** Standard admission is available through platforms like Trip.com, GetYourGuide, and Klook, often at a discount compared to walk-in prices. Optional VR game and 9D film add-ons are available for an extra fee.

**Time needed:** Most visitors spend 60 to 90 minutes exploring all 27 rooms.

## Who Is It For?

Space & Time Cube+ works well for couples, families with children, and solo travellers looking for something beyond the usual temple circuit. It is also a solid rainy-day activity since the entire experience is indoors and air-conditioned.

The museum represents Bangkok's growing reputation as a hub for immersive art experiences alongside Cave Fantasy at MBK Center and the seasonal Bangkok Art Biennale. For travellers who appreciate the intersection of technology and creativity, it is a worthwhile stop in 2026.
