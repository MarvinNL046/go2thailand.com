## Phuket's Beach Club Boom

Phuket has long been Thailand's nightlife capital, but 2026 has taken things to a different level. A wave of ambitious new beach clubs has opened across the island, each competing to offer something visitors have never seen before. Whether you want a laid-back sunset session or a full-blown floating party in the Andaman Sea, there is now a club for every mood.

Here are four beach clubs worth planning your [Phuket trip](/blog/phuket-travel-guide-2026/) around.

## Yona — The World's First Floating Beach Club

Yona is not on a beach at all. It is a three-level floating platform anchored in the Andaman Sea, accessible by a short boat transfer from Royal Phuket Marina on the island's east coast. The platform features a 22-metre infinity pool, a lounge pool on the sunset terrace, a rooftop with panoramic ocean views, four bars, and a full restaurant.

Mornings at Yona are family-friendly and relaxed. From 2pm onwards the venue switches to an adults-only party mode (age 20 and up) with resident DJs and live entertainment. Tickets can be booked through GetYourGuide and include the boat transfer. For context on the island's full entertainment offer, see our guide to [Phuket's best attractions](/city/phuket/attractions/).

## Barra Cuda — Thailand's First Pool Dance Floor

Located just 400 metres from [Bangla Road](/blog/bangla-road-phuket-digital-makeover-nightlife-2026/) on [Patong Beach](/city/phuket/attractions/patong-beach/), Barra Cuda spans 3,000 square metres with two pools, VIP cabanas, and direct beachfront access. Its headline feature is Thailand's first-ever pool dance floor, which turns the main pool into a party venue after dark.

In early 2026, O Beach Ibiza ran a series of Thursday takeover events here, bringing the famous Ibiza pool party format to Phuket for the first time. Expect more international DJ residencies throughout the year.

## Catch Beach Club — The Established Favourite

Catch Beach Club on Bang Tao Beach remains the most popular choice among Phuket's beach clubs for good reason. The venue offers comfortable cabanas and loungers, an infinity pool with panoramic views of white sand and turquoise water, signature cocktails, and a refined food menu.

Bang Tao Beach alone now hosts more than ten beach clubs, including [RAVA](/blog/rava-beach-club-phuket-bangtao-longest-beach-club-2026/) (Thailand's longest beach club at 150 metres with three infinity pools), Xana, and Nomads. Catch stands out for its consistent quality and prime location.

## Fira — Mediterranean Vibes Above Kata Noi

Fira Beach Club takes a different approach. Set high above [Kata Beach](/city/phuket/attractions/kata-beach/) in southern Phuket, this rooftop venue combines Mediterranean-style dining with sunset DJ sessions. Entry starts at around 1,000 THB, which is redeemable against food and drinks.

The atmosphere is stylish but relaxed, making Fira a strong choice for couples or small groups who want the beach club experience without the heavy party energy.
