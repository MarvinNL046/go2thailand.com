## Bangkok: Southeast Asia's K-pop Capital

Bangkok has become one of the most important cities on the global K-pop concert circuit. Major groups consistently include Thailand's capital on their world tours, and 2026 is shaping up to be a record year with some of the biggest names in Korean pop performing across multiple months.

If you are a K-pop fan planning a trip to Thailand, here is every confirmed concert you need to know about.

## Confirmed K-pop Concerts in Bangkok 2026

### SEVENTEEN — NEW_ World Tour
- **Dates:** March 14-15, 2026
- **Venue:** Suphachalasai National Stadium
- **What to expect:** The 13-member group brings their NEW_ World Tour to Bangkok as one of its key Southeast Asian stops. Known for their powerful choreography and self-produced music, SEVENTEEN consistently sells out stadiums across the region.

### TREASURE — PULSE ON Tour
- **Date:** May 16, 2026
- **Venue:** Suphachalasai National Stadium
- **What to expect:** TREASURE returns to Thailand after two years with their PULSE ON World Tour. Fans can look forward to live debuts of tracks from their latest mini-albums "PLEASURE" and "LOVE PULSE", including "Paradise", "Everything", "Yellow" and "Saruru", alongside fan favourites like "Boy", "Jikjin" and "Bona Bona".

### BTS — ARIRANG World Tour
- **Dates:** December 3, 5 and 6, 2026
- **Venue:** To be confirmed
- **What to expect:** The biggest K-pop event of the year. BTS is officially back on the global stage with their long-awaited ARIRANG World Tour, spanning 79 dates across 34 cities in 23 countries. The Bangkok stop will be one of the most in-demand concert events in Thailand this decade. Expect tickets to sell out within minutes.

## Tips for Attending K-pop Concerts in Bangkok

**Tickets sell fast.** Major acts like BTS and SEVENTEEN typically sell out during presale rounds. Sign up for fan club memberships and follow official ticketing platforms like Thai Ticket Major and Live Nation Tero for announcements.

**Book accommodation early.** Concert weekends push hotel prices up across central Bangkok, especially near the National Stadium area in Pathum Wan. Booking two to three months ahead can save you significant money.

**Transport.** Suphachalasai National Stadium is accessible via BTS Skytrain (National Stadium station). After concerts, expect heavy crowds at the station — consider walking to nearby Siam station instead or using a ride-hailing app from a side street.

**Stay longer.** Many international fans fly to Bangkok specifically for concerts. Use the trip as an excuse to explore the city for a few extra days — the street food, temples and nightlife are worth it.

## More to Come

Additional K-pop events including SMTOWN LIVE and other fan meetings are expected to be announced throughout 2026. Bangkok's position as a regional concert hub means there will likely be more additions to the schedule as the year progresses.

Keep checking official channels and ticketing platforms for updates. Thailand's K-pop scene shows no signs of slowing down.
