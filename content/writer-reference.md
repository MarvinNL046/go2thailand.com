# Writer Reference — Widget & Affiliate Guide

## Available Widgets

Place these HTML comments in your blog post where you want a widget to appear.
Each widget will be automatically replaced with a styled interactive box.

### Accommodation Widget
`<!-- WIDGET:booking -->`
Use after mentioning hotels, hostels, resorts, or where to stay.

### Tours & Activities Widget
`<!-- WIDGET:klook -->`
Use after mentioning cooking classes, day trips, snorkeling, island tours, etc.

### Guided Tours Widget
`<!-- WIDGET:getyourguide -->`
Use after mentioning walking tours, food tours, cultural experiences.

### Transport Widget
`<!-- WIDGET:12go -->`
Use after mentioning buses, trains, ferries, or getting between cities.

### eSIM / Connectivity Widget
`<!-- WIDGET:saily -->`
Use after mentioning SIM cards, internet, staying connected, or data plans.

### Flights Widget
`<!-- WIDGET:trip -->`
Use after mentioning flights, airports, or flying to/within Thailand.

### Pro Tip Box (non-affiliate)
`<!-- WIDGET:tip:Your tip text here -->`
Use for practical travel tips, warnings, or insider advice.

## Widget Placement Rules

1. Place 3-5 widgets throughout the article
2. Space them evenly — never two widgets back-to-back
3. Place widgets AFTER a relevant paragraph, BEFORE the next H2
4. Every article should have at minimum: 1x booking, 1x activity (klook or getyourguide), and 1 other
5. The tip widget can be used 1-2 times for non-commercial practical advice
