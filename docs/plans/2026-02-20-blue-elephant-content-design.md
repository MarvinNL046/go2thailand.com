### Source Material

YouTube video transcript from The Roaming Cook (Gary) visiting Blue Elephant Royal Thai Cooking School Bangkok. Key facts extracted:
- 100+ year old heritage building
- Chef Nor: owner, 40 years experience, award-winning
- Chef Chai: demonstration chef
- Krishna: market guide, ingredient expert
- Market: Samyan Market (normally Bangrak Market)
- Dishes: Thai green curry, golden bags, chicken cashew nut, pad thai
- Australian tenderloin beef used for curry
- Blue Elephant curry pastes available in UK
- Also has location in Phuket
- Beginner-level course
- Includes recipe booklet, apron, certificate
- They fry/grill some dishes for you (golden bags, chicken sate)
